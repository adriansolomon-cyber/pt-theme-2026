Garden offices, built to last

A Project Timber garden office turns unused garden space into a proper room you can use every day of the year. Our fully-insulated ranges — insulated in the walls, floor and roof, with double glazing as standard — stay warm in winter and cool in summer, so there's no "summer-only" compromise. Composite and cedar cladding options keep maintenance low and looks high, while pressure-treated timber buildings are backed by our up-to-25-year anti-rot guarantee.

Every garden office is designed and made in Britain at our Nottinghamshire workshop and built from pre-assembled, pre-insulated panels — so most of the work is done before it arrives. Configure your size and finish online to see your price instantly, add an assembly service if you'd prefer us to build it. Whether you need a quiet home office, a studio, a gym or a guest room, there's a size and specification to suit.
